EXERCISE 2 REPORT - CHAOS IN THE LOGISTIC MAP
=============================================

Equation used:
x(n+1) = r * x(n) * (1 - x(n))

Conclusion:
The population growth becomes chaotic at approximately r = 3.57.
A more precise value for the onset of chaos is r = 3.56995.

Explanation:
For small values of r, the population goes to a stable value.
When r increases, the system begins to oscillate between 2 values,
then 4 values, then 8 values, and so on.
After about r = 3.57, the behavior becomes chaotic.
This means the population no longer settles into a simple repeating pattern.
